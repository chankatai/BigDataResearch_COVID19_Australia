About the COVID-19 of Australia

The first confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Australia were reported in late January 2020. This page provides a number of visual representations of information about COVID-19 in Australia.Our website mainly displays information about New South Wales and Victoria.

New South Wales

Current Alerts

As of 3pm on 29 January 2021, the last Local Government Area in NSW, Cumberland, was removed as a COVID-19 affected area. New South Wales currently has no COVID-19 affected areas.

Victoria

A COVID-19 concerns notice is in effect for Victoria. Greater Melbourne has been identified as an affected area and there are places of high concern. The COVID-19 concerns notice affects anyone entering NSW on or after 6am Wednesday 12 May who has been in Greater Melbourne in the previous 14 days who has entered NSW and has been in Greater Melbourne at a place of high concern (close contact venue or casual contact venue).
